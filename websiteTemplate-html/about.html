About Us

Innovation Through Hands-On Robotics

Our Mission

The St. Clair Robotics Club is a student-led organization focused on exploring robotics through practical, hands-on projects. We design and build robots that merge mechanical design, electronics, and software.

Our members gain experience with microcontrollers, CAD, PCB design, and programming while bringing creative ideas to life in a collaborative environment.
