About Us

Innovation Through Hands-On Robotics

Our Mission

The St. Clair Robotics Club is a student-led organization focused on exploring robotics through practical, hands-on projects. We design and build robots that merge mechanical design, electronics, and software.

Our members gain experience with microcontrollers, CAD, PCB design, and programming while bringing creative ideas to life in a collaborative environment.

What We Do

Robot Design & Build

Design, prototype, and build functional robots from concept to completion using CAD software and fabrication tools.

Electronics & PCB Design

Learn circuit design, PCB layout, and soldering techniques to create custom electronics for robotics applications.

Programming & Software

Develop software for microcontrollers, implement algorithms, and program autonomous robot behavior.

Our Values

Hands-On Learning

Learn by doing through practical projects and real-world applications.

Collaboration

Work together in teams to solve complex engineering challenges.

Innovation

Push boundaries and explore creative solutions to robotics problems.

Inclusivity

Welcome students of all skill levels and backgrounds to learn and grow.
