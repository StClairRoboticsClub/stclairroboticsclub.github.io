Join the Club

Become part of our innovative robotics community at St. Clair College

How to Join

01

Join Our Discord

Connect with our community on Discord where we coordinate meetings, share updates, and collaborate on projects.

Join Discord
02

Attend a Meeting

Come to one of our weekly meetings to meet the team, see what we're working on, and learn about ongoing projects. All skill levels welcome!

03

Get Involved

Jump into a project that interests you, attend workshops, or start your own initiative. We provide mentorship and resources to help you succeed.

04

Build & Learn

Work on real robots, learn from experienced members, and build skills that will last a lifetime.
