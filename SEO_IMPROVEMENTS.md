# SEO Improvements Summary

This document outlines the SEO improvements made to help your website rank better in Google search results.

### 1. **robots.txt File**
- Created `/docs/robots.txt` to guide search engine crawlers
- Allows all crawlers to index your site
- Points to your sitemap location

### 2. **sitemap.xml File**
- Created `/docs/sitemap.xml` with all your pages
- Helps Google discover and index all pages faster
- Includes priority and change frequency for each page

### 3. **Enhanced Meta Tags**
Added to all pages:
- **Keywords meta tags** - Relevant search terms for your club
- **Author meta tags** - Identifies content creator
- **Robots meta tags** - Explicitly tells search engines to index and follow links

### 4. **Open Graph & Twitter Cards**
- Added complete Open Graph tags to `about.html` and `team.html` (already existed on other pages)
- Ensures proper previews when shared on social media
- Improves click-through rates from social platforms

### 5. **Canonical URLs**
- Added canonical tags to `about.html` and `team.html` (already existed on other pages)
- Prevents duplicate content issues
- Helps consolidate page authority

### 6. **Structured Data (JSON-LD)**
- Added Organization schema markup to all pages
- Helps Google understand your organization better
- Can enable rich snippets in search results
- Includes:
  - Organization name, description, logo
  - Location (Windsor, ON)
  - Social media links
  - Contact information
